A simple callout that networks become more valuable as their connections have:
1. more frequency
2. more bandwidth

Such as the Mail > Email > Telegram > Whatsapp > Twitter
- Of increasing frequency

Or the Facebook > Instagram > Snapchat > TikTok
- Of increasing bandwidth from text to images to video

This framing makes the VR/AR conversion seem almost inevitable.
